Our Story

Founded in 2018, GrabASeat emerged from a simple vision to make movie ticket booking effortless and enjoyable for everyone. What started as a small initiative has now grown into India's most loved ticket booking platform.

We believe that entertainment should be accessible, affordable, and hassle-free. Our platform connects movie lovers with their favorite cinemas, offering the best seats at competitive prices.
